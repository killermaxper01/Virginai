Help & Support

Everything you need to use VirginAI effectively

How to Use VirginAI

  • Type your question in the chat input box
  • Press Enter for a new line (multiline support)
  • Press Ctrl + Enter to send your message
  • You can write long prompts, code, or math step-by-step

Multiline & Matrix Typing

VirginAI supports ChatGPT-style formatting. You can:

  • Write questions in paragraphs
  • Type equations, matrices, and formulas line by line
  • Paste code or structured text safely

Is VirginAI Free?

Yes. VirginAI is free to use with fair usage limits to ensure availability for all users. Heavy or abusive usage may be temporarily restricted.

Why Didn’t I Get an Answer?

Sometimes AI providers may be busy or temporarily unavailable.

  • Wait a few seconds and try again
  • Simplify or rephrase your question
  • Avoid sending repeated messages too quickly

Accuracy Disclaimer

VirginAI responses are generated by artificial intelligence. While we aim for accuracy, answers may occasionally be incorrect or incomplete. Always verify critical information.

Need More Help?

If you experience ongoing issues, please check back later. VirginAI is continuously improving to provide faster, smarter, and more reliable responses.
