Help & Usage

How to use VirginAI

  1. Type your question in the input box
  2. Press Enter or tap the send button
  3. Wait for the AI response

Tips

  • Ask clear questions for better answers
  • You can ask about learning, concepts, or general knowledge
  • Responses may take a few seconds during high traffic

Issues

If the AI does not respond, please refresh the page and try again.
