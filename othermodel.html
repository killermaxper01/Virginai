How to Switch Models

To switch between different AI models:

  1. Open the VirginAI chat interface
  2. Click on the model selector in the top right corner
  3. Choose your preferred AI model from the dropdown
  4. Start chatting - the model switch happens instantly

Note: Switching models may start a new conversation context. Some models are only available in Pro and Team plans.

Models may have varying availability. Response times and accuracy can differ between models.
