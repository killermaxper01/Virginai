VirginAI ("we", "our", or "the Service") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use VirginAI.

2. Information We Collect

VirginAI does not require users to create accounts or provide personal identification information.

  • Chat messages may be processed temporarily to generate responses.

3. How Data Is Used

Any data processed by VirginAI is used solely to:

Chat data may be stored temporarily in active sessions to maintain conversation context. This data is automatically cleared and is not retained long-term.

VirginAI does not permanently store personal conversations.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures such as rate limiting, restricted API access, and secure communication to protect the Service from misuse.

7. Children’s Privacy

VirginAI is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of VirginAI after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
