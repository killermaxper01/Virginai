About the Developer

VirginAI is developed by an independent developer focused on building secure, fast, and reliable AI-powered applications. The project is designed with a strong emphasis on privacy, performance, and clean user experience.

Technology Stack

VirginAI is powered by a modern, scalable, and carefully selected technology stack:

  • Backend: Python, Flask
  • AI Models: Google Gemini, Groq LLaMA (8B / 70B variants)
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla)
  • Security: Rate limiting, session isolation, CORS protection
  • Infrastructure: Render Cloud Hosting

Mission

The mission of VirginAI is to provide a clean, intelligent, and trustworthy AI assistant that helps users learn, explore ideas, and solve problems — without unnecessary complexity or invasive data practices.
